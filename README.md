# Family Tree Visualization App

The **Family Tree Visualization App** is a web application designed to help users visualize and explore their family connections intuitively and interactively. With this app, users can create, view, and edit family trees, making it easy to understand relationships and connections between family members.

## Key Features:

- **Interactive Visualization:** The app provides an interactive visualization of family trees, allowing users to navigate through generations and explore relationships effortlessly.
- **Customizable Nodes:** Users can customize nodes representing family members with personalized information such as name, date of birth, gender, address, email, and phone number.
- **Dynamic Connections:** Family connections are dynamically represented with edges between nodes, making it easy to understand familial relationships at a glance.
- **Drag-and-Drop Functionality:** Users can easily add, move, and rearrange family members within the tree using intuitive drag-and-drop functionality.
- **Expandable Nodes:** Nodes can be expanded to display detailed information about individual family members, including spouses, parents, children, and siblings.
- **Responsive Design:** The app is designed with a responsive layout, ensuring optimal viewing and usability across devices of all sizes, including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones.
- **User-Friendly Interface:** The user interface is intuitive and user-friendly, making it accessible to users of all ages and technical abilities.

With the **Family Tree Visualization App**, users can gain deeper insights into their family history, strengthen connections with relatives, and preserve their familial legacy for generations to come. Whether used for personal genealogy research or simply to better understand one's family structure, this app provides a powerful tool for exploring and celebrating the ties that bind us together.
